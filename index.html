The Painting of friendship

In this world of deceit, some bonds are formed
A treasure called friendship is truly adorned
With strokes of trust and different colours of laughter's embrace
A painting of friendship, let me now trace

Like the weekends beats, friendship rings
A melody of souls that joyously sings
Through phases of life, together we stride
In Times of triumph or when tears we hide

A friend is a shelter in the pouring rain
A beacon of light in the darkest terrain
Growing together, like crops on a farm
Together, we find solace and calm

With understanding eyes they see our plight
After a few roasts and laughs, comes the guiding light
Through trials and tests, they never depart
Just their presence soothes the heart

Through many gmeets, memories were made
In playful banters and fights unswayed
They celebrate our victories, without envy or strife
In the drawing of friendship the colors are rife

They'll lift you up when spirits are low
Chess valorant and backchod discussions to forget your woe
They encourage and inspire to always grow
They'll stand as a pillar, unwavering and strong
As companions who'll stand by me lifelong
Support me irrespective I'm right or wrong
Because of friendship we find strength anew
Union of souls both old and true
For in journey of life we are blessed
With friends by our side, comfortably/uncomfortably caressed

So let us cherish, these bonds we hold
In this world so cold, they're more valuable than gold
In future together we create chaos abound
The painting of friendship, this poem expounds
That this is a treasure forever renowned

Look me in the eye

When you don't look me in the eye
Could you tell me the reason why
Is it that you don't know what to say
Or you just don't like me anyway

The train , the street
The strangers that greet
Are better than the friends who looked at their feet
And later when we actually meet
They will always deny
That they didn't see me when they didn't look me in the eye

Friends or foe, real or fake,
The ones that behave differently for an advantage's sake
Some want to impress
While others seek to oppress
But amidst the charades and games they play
I long for honesty, genuine display

Yet, I find solace in the authenticity rare
In those who meet my gaze without a care
For they reveal a truth beyond disguise
A connection forged, where trust can rise

So, let them avert their eyes and pretend
While I seek the companions who comprehend
That true friendship is not about pretense
But rather about trust, love, and immense

In the end, those who cannot meet my stare
Are merely shadows, fleeting and unaware
For the ones who truly see, deep and wide
Are the ones who'll forever stand by my side

So, if you don't look me in the eye
Know that it's your loss, not mine, oh why?
For I'll continue to seek those who see
The beauty within, the real you and me

My First Trek

It was a very dark night
I was nervous with fright
And excitement was not in check
I was going for my first trek

It was another thing I didn't want to try
As I am generally too afraid even to cry
My cousin and my friend convinced me to come
It was better than sitting at home like a bag of scum

There were 20 people 4 guides
We began the journey with small strides
A few moments later we stopped for introduction somewhere
A few moments later I realized I was the youngest person there

We all walked uphill with torches in our hands
We hopped our way through the muddy strands
Few hours later I was completely tired
But A few snacks and the first sight of the peak got me all fired
It felt very good with friends around
And the icing on the cake was nature all around
It was still pretty dark
But I saw something shining on the trees
Fireflies they were, glowing with spark
The experience was even more mesmerizing, because of the gentle breeze

As we moved forward, there was dryness in my throat
Name of the place was Kalavantin fort
Drinking some water was so much better
As every sip made our bags so much lighter

As time passed the peak came closer
The real difficult part had begun
I was very scared but i didn't want to be a choker
But I couldn't think for long as I needed to reach the top before coming up of the sun

It was a very steep rocky climb
To top it off there was nothing on either side
The difference were the people around
Who encouraged and helped me to climb
Safe and sound
The guides stood on the edge
Held my hand on every step
Not afraid of getting hurt by a sharp hedge
Helping the unexperienced like Johny depp

Finally on the top, there was straight patch
We had to climb vertically up using a rope tied on the top
Can't say the mission was accomplished without a scratch
After having breakfast, I realised this was not the stop

As the sun began to rise, casting its golden glow
We beheld a breathtaking sight from the peak's plateau
The world unfolded before our eyes, vast and grand
Mountains, valleys, and a panoramic view of the land

With a sense of triumph, we embraced the moment
Our fears conquered, our limits stretched and bent
The first trek had transformed into a journey of growth
Pushing boundaries, discovering strength we didn't know

As we made our descent, with memories in our hearts
A newfound confidence in us, like a work of art
The dark night had paved the way for a beautiful day
And the lessons learned would forever stay

For even in the darkest of nights, there's a spark
Waiting to ignite, guiding us through the dark
And so, with gratitude and a newfound zest
We left the mountain, knowing we had truly been blessed

PACE

It was the break of dawn
My mother came in my room and turned the lights on
She said get up son,you need to go
You're already late ,don't be slow

It was the begining of a new journey
From school to college, a path of new discovery
My father said these are the most important years of your life
More difficult than cutting a log of wood with a pocketknife

You'll have to study a lot said my mother
You won't get time to play said my brother
My older friends said that you will suffer
All these thoughts made me stutter

Going to PACE ,on my way
A classmate told me you have to study 18 hours a day
10 lakh students dreamt with their mind and soul
Getting into IIT was their main life goal

The total seats were ten thousand
Better was the return ratio of equity based mutual fund
Getting less marks made me feel sad
The sine thetas and cos thetas made me go mad

All those bonds,all those atoms
All those pulleys,all those free body diagrams
All these things made my head spin
I always felt I was in a battle I could never win

My friends ,my teachers and my family of course
Their motivation my only energy source
That was what kept me going
There should've been gratefulness which I wasn't showing

Not being able to enjoy, made me grumpy
Not getting sound sleep,made me sloppy
These were things that gave me headaches
These were the reasons i made mistakes

Now I am doing engineering , obviously not from IIT
Said that just to kill your anxiety
Now when go back through those memories
The stories just make me freeze

Never had I thought ,solving a sum would make me joyful
And now I realise my teachers were so helpful
Talking to friends for hours,to clear doubts of course
Doing integrations and calculating the magnitude of force

These days have taught a lot to me
That life will never be easy
Difficult was my life is something even I see
But these were the days worth the sweat These are the days I won't forget
And these are the days I won't regret.

A Farmer's Story

One fine day
I was walking along the bay
Near a temple on the road I saw a man pray
His face sunk in dismay

I asked him "hello sir , what is your pain?"
He said " since a long time my village has seen no rain"
"All the sown seeds will go in vain
And all the hard work down the drain"

Assuring him I said" it will rain there soon"
I started to move as it was a hot noon
Then I heard him hum a sad tune
Something about khaleri and the monsoon

From khaleri I came to borrow another loan
Need to talk to my family ,can I borrow your phone
To tell them that I was denied
From the side of his eye he cried

I left after he was done
He cried a lot, not at all fun
Next Day, along the bay
Near the temple a body lay

Killed he was because of nature's wrrath
dense white froth emerged from his mouth
On the side of his coat
There was a small note

It said please send this money to my family
So they can live the next few days happily
Inside there were 15000 rupees
That wasn't even 1/10 th of my fees

With the money there was a receipt of the loan
The loan was so small
From my eye it made a tear fall
More was the cost of my phone

Told this and asked help from my father
And gathered all I could gather
Requested help from a local farmer's help fund
And sent to khaleri a sum of one lakh and fifty thousand.

A Joke

My life was at an all time low
So I decided to do a show
It was about comedy and foolery
Better than sitting at home and watching the traffic scenery

Let me tell you something folks
I Started it with some jokes
The people were stingy with applause
Maybe because the jokes were cringy of course

But once I got in my groove
Still the hands of the audience didn't move
Towards the middle of the show there was dead silence
My confidence and my self esteem was on the fence

But the show had to finish
As my money had started to diminish
Unemployed I was
Being a moderately qualified engineer was the cause

Stand up comedy was the only occupation left
Apart from robbery,crime and theft
Maybe people don't know engineering has this darkside
Which creates thoughts like that of suicide

The Battle of the Boy and the Bear

Once in a jungle there was a mighty bear
So big he was all covered in black hair
Furious he looked and was so gall
In a bite,a man he could maul

Ben's brother, a victim of the Bear's rage
None could every have thought the best was so Savage
So he went around in the village asking for help
Nothing did he hear apart from a dog's Yelp

So he went,to take on the bear alone Without thinking twice he entered the danger zone To kill the bear he had planned a trap But the bear was smart,OH CRAP!!

Ripped into pieces the boy was
Gave up his life for a worthy cause
He tried to improve the life of the people of the village
And to make peace with his dead brothers image

Rise ,we must all
Help the person trying to make a change
Before he crumbles and falls
And we continue living on the same page
